# Fried Rice



#### Ingredients
	* 16 oz dry cornbread mix
	* 1 small onion (chopped)
	* 2 tbsp butter
	* 0.5 cups celery (chopped)
	* 8-10 pieces bacon
	* 2 eggs
	* 2 cup chicken stock
	* 2 tbsp sage
	* salt/pepper to taste

#### Directions
	1. cook bacon
	2. cook onion/celery in bacon grease
	3. add sage to stuffing mix
	4. dip bread in mixture
	5. fry till golden brown
